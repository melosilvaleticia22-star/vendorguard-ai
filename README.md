# VendorGuard AI – Enterprise SaaS Blueprint

VendorGuard AI is an enterprise-focused AI solution designed to help companies assess vendor risk and compliance before onboarding or renewal.

## Problem
Enterprises face legal, regulatory, data privacy, and ESG risks when working with third-party vendors. Manual reviews are slow, expensive, and inconsistent.

## Solution
VendorGuard AI uses artificial intelligence to analyze vendor contracts and documentation, identifying:
- Compliance gaps
- Legal risks
- Data protection issues
- Financial and operational risks
- ESG and reputational red flags

## What’s Included
- Core AI prompt logic for vendor risk analysis
- Enterprise risk report template
- Product documentation and usage guide
- Pricing strategy for high-ticket B2B SaaS
- Go-to-market strategy
- Proof-of-concept demo assets

## Target Market
- Mid-market and enterprise companies
- Procurement teams
- Compliance and legal departments

## Status
This is a pre-revenue MVP and enterprise SaaS blueprint.
No backend or proprietary code is included.
